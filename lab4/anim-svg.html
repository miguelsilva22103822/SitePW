Poema

There,

Sun birds chipper,

Their feathers, light lime,

Seep in the sunshine.

Mango trees

Summit and soar,

Stalk high above

The forest floor.

Where

A Bengal tiger,

Obsolete

As an emperor

Trembles

As the hushed wind-

Breathes -
